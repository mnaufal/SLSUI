About Google Integration (New)

  1. SLS has built deeper integration with Google Suite of Tools.

  2. Students are encouraged to link their iCON 2.0 or Google accounts with SLS.

  3. Teachers will attach Google Files to the pre-populated student answer for students to access/attempt. Students may access the file by clicking on the Open in New Tab or Edit button.

  4. When attempting to access the file, a personal copy of the file will be created for the student. The student’s name will be reflected at the front of the file name.

  5. After the file is created, students may view the new file in Google and edit it directly.

  6. After completing the work and returning to SLS, students will see the original file name has their name appended to the front. Students may then submit the file for teacher’s marking. Once submitted, students will not be able to edit their Google file.


Access Teacher’s Feedback

  1. After the teacher has given feedback on the student’s work, the student can edit a copy of their teacher’s comments under the Teacher Feedback tab to do corrections.

  2. The student’s corrections will be reflected in an updated Google document under the teacher’s feedback. A two-way communication can be established between teachers and students during the feedback process.
