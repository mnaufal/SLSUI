Insert Drawing

Respond with Drawing

  1. When editing a Rich Text-enabled component, choose the Drawing icon .

  2. You can choose to start with a Blank Drawing or Upload s Background Image.


Starting with Blank Drawing

  1. If you choose to start with a blank drawing, you will see a white canvas.

    • You may choose from 2 pen tip sizes by clicking on the pencil image for a thin line or the marker image for a thick line.

    • You may choose from 8 colours by clicking on the colour palette on the left.

    • To draw with a mouse, click the mouse and drag the cursor along.

    • To draw with your finger or stylus on a tablet, apply pressure on the screen while moving your finger or stylus.

    • To insert text, click the text box button and begin typing.

    • To erase or move any part of your drawing or text, click the Cursor icon and select the lines that you have drawn and click Delete.

  2. You can undo the most recent stroke by clicking Undo.

  3. You can clear all drawings by clicking Clear All.

  4. Click Add if you are done with the drawing. Your drawing will be inserted into the textbox.

  5. To edit the drawing, click the Edit icon to open up the drawing tool again.

  6. To delete the entire drawing, click the Delete icon.

  7. Upon clicking the Settings icon you can edit the drawing's:

    • Title

    • Description

    • Keyword Tags

  8. Once done, click Save.


Start with a Background Image

  1. If you choose to add a background image, you can either drag the file into the box or browse to choose the file from your computer.

  2. When the background image is uploaded, it will take up the full width of the drawing canvas. You will not be able to resize the image.

  3. You may draw over the background image using the pencil or marker.

  4. You may insert a text over the background image using the text box.

  5. When using the Clear All or Delete, only the drawings/text will be removed. The original background image will not be affected.

  6. Click Add if you are done with the drawing. Your drawing will be inserted into the textbox.
