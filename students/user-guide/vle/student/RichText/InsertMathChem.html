Insert Mathematical or Chemical Equations

Insert Mathematical Equation using MathType

  1. When editing a Rich Text-enabled component, choose the Mathematics Equation icon  .

  2. Use the toolbars found within the MathType editor or handwrite to create or edit the mathematical equation. For more information on the toolbars, click here.

  3. Click Insert in the MathType editor when done.

  4. You will be able to see the equation rendered as an image in your response.

  5. To edit an equation, double-click the image to open the MathType editor.

    6. Note: You are now able to copy and paste your mathematical equations in the Rich Text Editor.


Insert Mathematical Equation using LaTeX commands

  1. You can also type LaTeX equations between double dollar signs – $$ – in the Rich Text Editor. For more information on the LaTeX commands supported, click here.  

  2. The equation will be rendered as an image in the lesson preview.


Insert Chemical Equation using ChemType

  1. When editing a Rich Text-enabled component, choose the Chemical Equation icon .

  2. Use the toolbars found within the ChemType editor to create or edit the chemical equation. For more information on the toolbars, click here.

  3. Click Insert in the ChemType editor when done.

  4. You will be able to see the equation rendered as an image in your response.

  5. To edit an equation, double-click the image to open the ChemType editor.

    6. Note: You are now able to copy and paste your chemical equations in the Rich Text Editor.
