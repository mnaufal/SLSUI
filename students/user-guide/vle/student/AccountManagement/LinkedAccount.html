Manage Linked Account (New)

Users are able to associate their iCON 2.0 or Google account with SLS.

  1. Open the right menu and click Edit Profile.

  2. On the Profile page, click Manage Linked Account under Profile.

  3. Click Link Account.
  4. In the window that pops up, enter the email and password of the account you wish to connect to the SLS account.
  5. Check the box that allows ufinity.com to access Google Drive files. Select Continue.

  6. You have successfully linked the iCON 2.0/Google Account to SLS.
