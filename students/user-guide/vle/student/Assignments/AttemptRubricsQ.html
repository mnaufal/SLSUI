Attempt a Question with Rubrics

Rubrics can be added to Free-Response and Audio-Response Questions. These rubrics are meant for reference to understand the assessment criteria and improve one's performance.

  1. In a Free-Response or Audio-Response Question, click the Marks Range to expand the rubrics.

  2. You will then be able to see the assessment criteria and attainment levels.

  3. These can be used to guide you in answering the questions.
