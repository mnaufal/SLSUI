Set Assignment as Assessment

  1. From the Assignment Card, click Monitor. Alternatively, enter the Assignment and click the Monitoring icon in the header.

  2. Open the right menu and select Manage Assignment Settings.

  3. On the Assignment Settings subpage, click the Others tab.

  4. Select the checkbox beside Set as assessment.

Note:

  • Students will not be able to access embedded links or apps in the assignment unless they have been whitelisted for use in the assessment's network.

  • Students will not be able to view Assessments on their Assignments tab from the homepage or Assignments page. You will have to provide them with the Assessment URL for them to gain access to it.

DID YOU KNOW?
If you do not wish to create an Assessment (which will require the whitelisting of embedded links or apps), you may wish to set a Passcode to an Assignment. Students will require the Passcode before they can attempt the Assignment.
