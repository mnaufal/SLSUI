View Assignments

Assignments can be converted into Assessments. Assessment Cards are differentiated with a beige background.

Starting from R18, you will be able to view Print-friendly Worksheets.

Note: Students will not be able to view the Assessments on their Assignments page. They can only access the Assessments through the Student Attempt URL.

View New Assignments

Assignments that have been recently assigned (up to 3 days) will have a New label on the Assignment Card.


Assignment Cards

  1. The progress bar in the Assignment Cards shows the completion status of students in the Class Group

    • Yellow portion represents the proportion of students who have not started the assignment

    • Blue portion represents the proportion of students who have completed the assignment.

    • Red portion represents the proportion of students whose assignments are overdue.

  2. Lesson Assignment Cards and Course Assignment Cards are indicated differently in the Assignment List. Course Assignment Cards show the next available Section or Section where action is pending.

    3. Note: Students will see all their assignments (except assessments) on the Assignments tab on the homepage if they are incomplete/overdue. Overdue assignments remain accessible to students.


View All Assignments

  1. To view all Assignments, you can enter the Assignment List page by either:

    • clicking Assignments on the homepage under the Assignments tab, or

    • clicking Assignments found from the left menu.

  2. You may search for Assignments on the Assignments page by entering the title in the Search Bar. You may apply the Assignment Status and Class Group filters. Click Apply to perform your selection.

  3. For a more refined search, click Filter to filter the Assignments by Assignment Status, Class Group, Lesson/Course, and Marking Status (for Teacher-Marked Quizzes only). Click Apply to perform your selection.

  4. You can choose to view your Assignments in Card view or in List view .

  5. Assignments in List view are sorted according to the latest Start Date. You may also sort the assignments according to the Assignment Title, and Due Date. Assessments in List View will be indicated by an orange tag beside the Title.

DID YOU KNOW?

The Student Attempt URL can be shared with students so that they can access the assignment directly after logging in. You may use any URL shortener or QR code generator to make it easier for students to access the assignment. Teachers can find the Student Attempt URL by clicking the Overflow (3-dot icon) when inside an Assignment.
