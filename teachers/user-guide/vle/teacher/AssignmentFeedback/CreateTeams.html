Create Teams (New)

Starting from R18, Activity Teams has been introduced to replace Groupwork. Teachers can now create Teams to allow differentiated access and/or group learning.

Note: Game Teams is different from Activities Teams.

  1. From the Assignment Settings subpage, click the Activities / Teams tab.

  2. Select the checkbox(es) for the Activities you wish to create Teams for. You may create different Teams for different Activities, or simply apply Subgroups as Teams.


Apply Subgroups as Teams

  1. From the Activities / Teams tab, click Apply Subgroups as Teams.

  2. Select the checkbox beside the Subgroup you wish to add to Game Teams.

  3. Click Add to proceed.

  4. Your selected Subgroups will be shown on the Assignment Settings subpage.

  5. You can view or edit the Team by clicking View / Edit.

  6. To delete a Team, click the Overflow (3-dot icon), followed by Delete. Click Ok to confirm.

  7. Click Back to Assignment Settings to return to the Assignment subpage.

  8. Click Save, followed by X to return to the Assignment Summary page.

Note: To apply the Subgroups as Teams across multiple Activities, select the checkboxes beside the Activities and click Apply Subgroups as Teams in the table header.


Add New Teams

  1. From the Activities / Teams tab, click Add Team.

  2. Enter a Team Name, followed by Add Students to add students to Team.

  3. Select the checkbox beside the studentâ€™s Form Class to include the student in Team.

  4. When you are done, click Add.

  5. Click Save to confirm your action and Back to Game Teams to return to the Game Teams subpage.

  6. Your created Teams will be shown on the Assignment Settings subpage.

  7. You can view or edit the Team by clicking View / Edit.

  8. To delete a Team, click the Overflow (3-dot icon), followed by Delete. Click Ok to confirm.

  9. Click Back to Assignment Settings to return to the Assignment subpage.

  10. Click Save, followed by X to return to the Assignment Summary page.

Note: To create Teams across multiple Activities, select the checkboxes beside the Activities and click Add Team in the table header.


View Teams in Assignments

  1. In an Assignment, a Team Activity will be indicated with the Team icon beside the Activity Title.

  2. To view the Teams and List of Teammates, click the Team icon.
