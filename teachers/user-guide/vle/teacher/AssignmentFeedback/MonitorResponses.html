Monitor Students' Responses in an Assignment (Lesson)

Monitoring Assignment (Lesson)

You can monitor your students' Assignment progress on the Monitor Assignment page.


Monitor Assignment (Lesson) from the Heatmap

  1. From the Assignment Card, click Monitor. Alternatively, enter the Assignment and click the Monitoring icon in the header.

  2. The following information will be displayed on the Monitor Assignment page:

    • Assignment Status: based on the overall Completion Status of students assigned.

    • Download button: to download a CSV file of marks and/or responses.

    • Filter : to filter by Student's Assignment and Activity completion status.

    • Toggle on/off to show marks under Setting : to show/hide marks on the Heatmap.

    • Legend : legend for the colours and icons used in the Heatmap.

    • Heatmap: table of students and their response data. All questions will be indicated in this table, , with the Team icon indicating a Team Quiz Click the Activity icon or to navigate to an Activity. Alternatively, click the Filter icon beside the Activity icon to do so. Within an Activity, click the Question Number in the table header to view the aggregated students' responses for that particular question.

      Note:

      • You can filter your view by clicking the Filter icon. Select an Activity from the dropdown list, and filter the Assignment based on Assignment Status and Activity Status.

      • Clicking the Question Number on the Heatmap will redirect you to the Aggregated Student Responses via View All Responses.

  3. To exit the filtered view, click the X beside the Filter icon.


Monitor an Interactive Thinking Tool

  1. Under the Interactive Thinking Tool, click View Class Submissions to open the Interaction Board.

  2. For Interactive Thinking Tool, you can view responses for individual column or all the columns at once by choosing the View All tab.

  3. To view comments for any response, click Comments ( ) in the Response Card. The number of Comments is indicated in parenthesis.

  4. All Comments made by you and your students will be made visible, arranged according to the time stamp, with the most recent post at the bottom. Changes to the state of the comments (e.g. deleted or edited) are also reflected in the Comments.

    Note: Names of students who have left a comment cannot be hidden.

  5. To comment on a response, click Comments ( ), followed by the Add icon on the bottom right.

  6. After typing in the comment, click Post.

  7. You can bookmark an Interactive Thinking Tool Card by clicking the Bookmark icon . This will allow you to filter the Cards in the Interaction Board. Bookmarked Cards will also be displayed first.


Monitor a Poll

  1. Under the Poll, click View Class Submissions to open the Interaction Board.

  2. The results of the Poll will be displayed as a bar graph. Hovering over the individual bar graph will show the number of votes for a particular option.

  3. The legend for the Poll is found below the bar graph.
