About Lessons and Courses (Improved)

Lesson

A Lesson can contain up to 20 Activities or Quizzes. Each Activity comprises a number of Components such as Text/Media, Poll, Interactive Thinking Tool, and Questions.

A Lesson can be assigned to students as an Assignment.

The Lesson Plan will provide an overview of the entire lesson, which includes the number of Activities and Components.


Course

A Course can contain up to 20 Sections and each Section can contain up to 20 Activities or Quizzes. Each Activity comprises a number of Components such as Text/Media, Poll, Interactive Thinking Tool, and Questions.

A Course can be assigned to students as an Assignment.

Activity or Quiz

An Activity or Quiz is part of a Lesson or Course. Teachers can create up to 20 Activities or Quizzes in a Lesson or Section. An Activity can comprise of Components of all types, including Text/media, all question types, Poll, and Interactive Thinking Tool.

A Quiz can only contain questions. There are three types of Quiz mode:

  • Teacher-Marked Quiz: Students will receive their marks and individualised comments after Teacher has marked and released the Quiz.

  • Auto-graded Quiz: Students will receive their marks and feedback after completing the entire Quiz.

  • Progressive Quiz: Students will receive their marks and feedback after each Question in the Quiz.

To create an Activity or Quiz, you need to be in the Lesson Editor or Course Editor.

Note: Starting from R18, you can set an Activity as optional, and create Team Activities in Quizzes.


Lesson Details or Course Details Card

The Lesson Details or Course Details card can be found in the Settings drawer on the Cover page of every Lesson or Course. It contains:

  1. Lesson or Course Notes
  2. Language Settings
    • Language Localisation
    • E-Dictionary
  3. Tags
    • Keyword Tags
    • Learning Experiences (For Lessons only)
    • Lesson or Course Tags
  4. Lesson or Course Credited to..
  5. Lesson or Course Permissions (New in R18)

    • Allow teachers to copy, assign and publish this Lesson or Course; allow students to copy this Lesson or Course

    • Allow teachers and students to view print-friendly worksheet

    • Allow students to reattempt as self-study lesson or Course

    Note: By default, these permissions will be turned on for newly created Lessons or Courses.

  6. Lesson or Course Information

Note: You can expand or hide a Lesson Details or Course Details card by clicking the Settings icon .

Section Details Card (For Courses only)

The Section Details Card can be found in the Settings drawer on the Cover page of every Section. It contains:

  1. Section Notes
  2. Learning Experience

Note: You can view or hide a Section Details card by clicking the Settings icon .


Activity Details Card

The Activity Details card contains

  1. Activity Notes
  2. Active Learning Process (ALP)


Component Details Card

  1. A Component Details card is attached to every component in an Activity. These cards are arranged in order of the Components found in that Activity. To navigate to the component, you may also click on the card. This will bring you to the top of the Component.

  2. The Component Details Cards can also be edited when in Lesson Editor or Course Editor.

  3. Click Edit to include Questions into Learning Progress, add Keyword Tags for all components except Text/Media and ITT and Question Tags for questions only. These tags will help to improve your Search Result when searching for resources in Search.
