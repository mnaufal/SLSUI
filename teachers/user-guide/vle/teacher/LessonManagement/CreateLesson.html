Create a Lesson (New)

Starting from R18, by default, the checkbox beside Allow teachers to copy, assign and publish this Lesson; allow students to copy this Lesson will be checked. This is to allow you to assign the Lesson. You can do this from the Lesson Details subpage.

Create New Lesson

  1. On the homepage, select Create New Lesson or Course. Select New Lesson. You can create a Custom Lesson and set the number of Activities it will contain, use one of the My Personal Lesson Templates or use one of the eight Lesson Templates.

  2. To find out more about each template, you can click Find Out More when selecting from My Personal Lesson Templates or Lesson Templates.

  3. Click Create from Custom Lesson or Choose from one of the templates to proceed.

  4. In the Lesson Title subpage, enter the Lesson Title, followed by Create.

  5. The Lesson Plan subpage will display the number of Activities chosen or the template you selected. Click X to close the subpage.

  6. In the Lesson Editor, click the Pencil icon to enter the details for the Lesson:

    • Lesson Title - The Lesson Title will be pre-filled with the type of Lesson Template that you choose. For the Custom template, the default pre-filled Lesson Title is "Untitled". Change the Lesson Title to one that is concise and student-friendly.

    • Featured Image (optional) - The thumbnail of a Lesson is visible on the cover page. If this Lesson is submitted and featured in the MOE Library or Community Gallery, it will also be visible in the MOE Library or Community Gallery listing.

      Note:

      • The recommended size is 640px x 454 px (i.e. 3:2 ratio). If the image exceeds the dimensions, it will be cropped or fitted to the dimensions.

      • You can add a Featured Image from Library or your Starred Resources by clicking Add Existing.

    • Lesson Description (optional) - The description is visible to students on the cover page of an Assignment. You can include details such as learning objectives and success criteria.

  7. Click Save to save the main details of the Lesson. The Lesson Details Card can be expanded/hidden by clicking the Settings icon . Click Edit to edit the remaining details of the Lesson.

  8. You can add Activities within the Lesson. Click here to find out more.


Edit Lesson Details Card

  1. On the Lesson Cover page, the Lesson Details Card can be expanded/hidden by clicking the Settings icon .

  2. To edit the Lesson Details Card, click Edit on the Card. Alternatively, you can click the Overflow (3-dot icon) and select Edit Details. The following details can be edited:

    • Lesson Notes (optional) - The Notes are visible only to Teachers with whom the Lesson is shared.

    • Language Settings (Optional):

      • Language Localisation - Instructions and questions of assignments may be presented to students in either one of the four languages - English (default), Chinese, Malay and Tamil.

      • E-Dictionary (for Chinese only) - You can choose to allow students to use the Chinese Language or Chinese Language with English Translation E-Dictionary.

    • Tags

      • Keyword Tags (optional) - Keywords are used to improve the searchability of a resource.

      • Learning Experience - You can change the Learning Experience. However, your existing activities, if any, will remain unchanged.

      • Lesson Tags (optional) - Lessons can be tagged to learning objectives in the MOE Content Map to improve searches and recommendations in the Community Gallery.

    • Lesson Credited to.. (optional) - You can credit teachers who have contributed to the creation of the Lesson. Their names will be listed when your Lesson is published in the Community Gallery. This is different from teachers with whom the Lesson is shared. Teachers can be given viewing or editing rights via Sharing of Lesson, but may not have contributed to the Lesson.

    • Lesson Permissions (New in R18)

      • Allow teachers to copy, assign and publish this Lesson; allow students to copy this Lesson

      • Allow teachers and students to view print-friendly worksheet

      • Allow students to reattempt as self-study Lesson

  3. Click Save to proceed.


Create New Lesson from My Drive

You can also create a new Lesson by clicking Add New in My Drive, under My Lessons & Courses tab.

  1. Open the left menu. Expand the Resources dropdown list and select My Drive. By default, you will be in the My Lessons & Courses tab.

  2. Click Add New.

  3. Select New Lesson or Course from the menu. You can create a Custom Lesson or select a template under My Personal Lesson Template or Lesson Templates.

  4. Click Create from Custom Lesson or Choose from one of the templates to proceed.


Add an Activity (Improved)

After creating a new Lesson, you can start adding Activities. You can now set Activities as optional by selecting the checkbox beside Set Activity as optional. This can be found under Activity Title.

Note: You can add up to 20 activities in a Lesson.


Manage Passcode Settings (New)

You can add a passcode to your Lesson to help manage access to your Lesson. Students without the passcode will not be able to access the Lesson.

  1. From the Lesson Editor, open the right menu and click Manage Passcode Settings.

  2. In the Passcode Settings subpage, select the checkbox beside Enable passcode for this Lesson.

  3. Enter the passcode for this Lesson. Passcode is case-sensitive.

  4. Click Save when done and X to close the subpage.


Save New Lesson

Click the Done icon found at the top right of the page to save your new Lesson.


Preview a Lesson

  1. In the Lesson View mode, open the right menu and click Preview as Student.

  2. The Lesson will be previewed in a new tab.

  3. You can attempt the Lesson as a student.

  4. Click X at the top right to exit the Lesson Preview. Click Ok to confirm your action.
