Share a Lesson/Course

Share a Lesson/Course

You can share Lessons and Courses that you have created with other teachers for them to use or to co-design the Lesson/Course.

  1. Open the left menu. Expand the Resources dropdown list and select select My Drive. By default, you will be in the My Lessons & Courses tab.

  2. In the listing, click the Overflow (3-dot icon) for the resource you wish to share and click Share.

  3. You may also share a resource from the View Lesson/Course page. Open the right menu and select Share with Teachers.


Add Teachers to Share

  1. In the Add Teachers subpage, click Add to select teachers you wish the share the resource with.

  2. Search for the teachers by Name or Email. You can apply the school filter to narrow the search by School.

  3. Select the teachers by selecting the checkboxes on the left of their names. You may select more than one teacher across multiple pages.

  4. After you have selected the teachers, click Add.

  5. Select the permissions to give the collaborating teachers (Can View or Can Edit).

  6. Click Save and X to close the subpage.


Remove Teachers

  1. To remove any teacher from the list, click Delete under the Action column.

  2. Click Save to confirm.


Change Sharing Permissions

  1. To change sharing permissions for any teacher, open the Share with Teachers subpage.

  2. Click on the dropdown list next to each collaborator's name to update the permissions.

  3. Click Save to confirm.
