About Lesson/Section Templates

  1. When creating a new Lesson/Section, you can create a Custom Lesson/Section or use one of the eight Lesson/Section Templates:

    • Active Learning Processes

    • Acquisition

    • Discussion

    • Collaboration

    • Inquiry

    • Practice

    • Production

    • Assessment Templates by Subjects

  2. The Lesson/Section Templates are aligned to the Active Learning Processes and six Learning Experience types, and an e-Assessment template.

    After choosing one of the Lesson/Section Templates, the Learning Experience in the Lesson/Section Details Card will be filled for you. You can change the Learning Experience in the Lesson/Section Details Card on the new Lesson/Section page.

    Note: Your existing activities will remain unchanged.

  3. Depending on the Lesson/Section Template chosen, Activities will be pre-populated and tagged to the following Active Learning Processes (ALP):

    • Activate Learning

    • Promote Thinking and Discussion

    • Facilitate Demonstration of Learning

    A fourth ALP,Â Monitor and Provide Feedback, can be added to any Activity at any part of the Lesson/Course.

  4. You can add Activities to the Section/Lesson or edit the pre-populated Activities.

    Note: You can create up to a maximum of 20 Activities in a Lesson/Section.
