Make a Copy a Lesson/Course (Improved)

You can make a copy Lessons and Courses of SLS from the following places on SLS.

  1. Lessons and Courses from My Drive, which include:

    • Lessons and Courses that you created

    • Lessons and Courses that were shared with you (if permission has been given)

    • Lessons and Courses that were previously copied

  2. Lessons and Courses from the MOE Library (if permission has been given)

  3. Lessons and Courses from the Community Gallery

  4. Lessons and Courses that are assigned as Assignments (if permission has been given)


Make a Copy of an Assignment

Start by selecting the Assignment you want a copy of from your Assignment List or Assignments tab on the homepage.

  1. Click Open on the Lesson/Course Card.

  2. Open the right menu and click Copy to My Drive. If permission has been disabled, you will not be able to copy the Assignment.

  3. You can make a copy of a Lesson/Course from an Assignment by clicking the Overflow (3-dot icon) and select Copy Lesson/Course to My Drive.

    Note:

    • This is helpful if you are the co-teacher for a Class Group and the other teacher has assigned a Lesson/Course you want a copy of.

    • You have edited your Assignment and wish to have a copy of the edited Lesson or Course.
