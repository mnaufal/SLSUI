Tag a Lesson/Course

Lessons and Courses can be tagged to Subject, Level, Topics or Sub-topics in the MOE Content Map so as to improve searchability in Community Gallery, MOE Library, and My Drive.

If Lessons and Courses are submitted to the Community Gallery, tags will allow teachers to find the Lesson/Course using filters and relevant search terms.

  1. To start, click the Pencil icon found at the top right of the header to edit your Lesson/Course.

  2. On the Lesson/Course Cover page, the Lesson/Course Details Card can be expanded/hidden by clicking the Settings icon .

  3. To edit the Lesson/Course Details Card, click Edit on the Card. Alternatively, you can click the Overflow (3-dot icon) and select Edit Details.


Add Lesson/Course Tags

  1. Click the dropdown arrow located on the right of the Lesson/Course Tag field.

  2. You can choose to select topics from the teaching subjects assigned to you (Assigned Subjects), subjects you are following (Followed Subjects), or from any other subject in the MOE Content Map (Other Subjects).

  3. Select the Subject from the dropdown list.

  4. Select the Level from the dropdown list. This list is based on the Subject chosen.

  5. Select the Content Map (previously known as Syllabus).

  6. Select the checkboxes next to the Topics or Sub-topics that are relevant to this Lesson/Course. You can click the downward arrow to expand the topics in order to see the Sub-topics.

  7. Click the upward arrow on the Lesson/Course Tag field to close the selection.


Add More Lesson/Course Tags

  1. To add more Lesson/Course Tags, click Add Lesson/Course Tags.

  2. Multiple Lesson/Course Tags will be displayed in an accordion format.

  3. To expand or collapse the tag details for viewing, click the Lesson/Course Tag Title or on the upward/downward arrow. To remove the tag, click the Trash icon.

  4. Click Save to exit.
