Award XP, Game Story and Achievements Manually (New)

  1. Select a gamified Assignment Card and click Monitor to enter the Monitor Assignment page on the Assignment page. Alternatively, from the Assignments tab in the homepage, click Monitor on a gamified Assignment Card.

  2. On the Monitor Assignment page, click the Game Experience tab.

  3. Select the checkbox beside the name(s) of the students and click Award XP, Award Game Story or Award Achievement found in the header of the Leaderboard.

  4. To Award XP, in the Award XP subpage,

    • Enter the amount of XP to be awarded and Description of the awarded XP.

    • Click Save to proceed and OK to confirm your action.

      Note: This action cannot be undone.

  5. To Award Game Story/Achievement. in the respective subpage,

    • Select the Game Story/Achievement to be awarded to the student(s). To preview the Game Story/Achievement, click Preview.

    • Click Save to proceed and OK to confirm your action.

      Note: This action cannot be undone.
