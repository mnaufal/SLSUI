Add and View a Passcode (New)

Teachers can add a Passcode to Lessons or Courses to restrict studentsâ€™ access to these resources. Students are required to enter the Passcode (case-sensitive) when they click the Assignment Card.

Add a Passcode

  1. Open the left menu. Expand the Resources dropdown list and select My Drive. By default, you will be in the My Lessons & Courses tab. You may also access Lessons and Courses under Shared With Me.

  2. Click Open to open a Lesson/Course for viewing.

  3. Click the Pencil icon to enter the Lesson or Course Editor.

  4. Open the right menu and select Manage Passcode Settings.

  5. In the Passcode Settings subpage, select the checkbox beside Enable passcode for this lesson/course and enter the Passcode in the text box field.

    Note:

    • Passcode is case-sensitive.

    • Passcode will remain visible to you.

  6. Click Save to confirm and X to close the subpage.


View a Passcode

  1. You can view a Passcode from:

    • Lesson or Course View

    • Within an Assignment

  2. From the Lesson or Course View, open the right menu and select View Passcode Settings.

  3. From with an Assignment, open the right menu and select View Passcode Settings.

  4. The Passcode Settings subpage will appear, revealing the Passcode.

  5. Click X to close the subpage.
