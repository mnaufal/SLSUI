Add Rubrics to Audio- and Free-Response Questions

Add Rubrics

Rubrics can be added to Free-Response or Audio-Response Questions to assess students holistically on performance tasks.

  1. From a Free-Response or Audio-Response Question, click Add Rubrics.

  2. A maximum of 6 bands (columns) and 6 criteria (rows) can be added in a Rubrics. Click the Overflow (3-dot icon) to Add Band, Add Criterion, Delete Band, or Delete Criterion.

  3. Enter the following fields:

    • Title

    • Band Descriptor

    • Criterion

    • Mark Range

      Note: Please see below on how to use the marks of the rubrics for the question.

    • Band Description

    • Description

      Note: You may enter descriptions in the form of text, mathematical formulae, chemical formulae, and media.

  4. Click Save after you are done editing. You can then add Rubrics Notes by clicking Edit under the Rubrics Details Card. You can view/hide the Rubrics Details Card by clicking the Settings icon.


Use Marks of the Rubrics (For Teacher-Marked Quiz only)

  1. The rubrics can be used as a reference, but not be bound to the mark ranges for the question. To do so, ensure that the box beside Show and use rubrics marks is unchecked.

  2. Alternatively, the marks of the rubrics can be used for the question. To do so, check the box for Show and use rubrics marks. The total marks will be calculated based on the marks allocated by the teacher for each criterion in the rubrics. This will also impact students' quiz scores in a Teacher-Marked Quiz.
