Edit a Lesson/Course (Improved)

Open Lesson/Course page

You can edit Lessons and Courses that you have created or copied, as well as Lessons and Courses that were shared with you with editing permissions.

  1. Open the left menu. Expand the Resources dropdown list and select Owned by Me under My Drive. You may also access Lessons and Courses under Shared With Me.

  2. Click Open to open a Lesson/Course for viewing.

Note: Courses are indicated by the Course icon while Lessons are indicated by the Lesson icon .

Lesson/Course Editor Page (Improved)

  1. In the Lesson/Course View Mode, click the Pencil icon found at the top right of the header to edit the Lesson/Course.

  2. To edit a gamified course, click the Gamification icon found at the top right of the header. Click here to find out more about Gamification.

  3. Make the necessary changes to the Lesson/Course.

  4. You can now set Activities to be optional. Find out how you can do so from Create a Lesson

  5. You may also add a passcode to the Lesson/Course. Find out more from Create a Lesson

  6. Click the Done icon found at the top right of the header to save your changes.


Note: If the Lesson/Course was shared with you as view-only, you will have to make a copy of the Lesson/Course before you can make changes.
