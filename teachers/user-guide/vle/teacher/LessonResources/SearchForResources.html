Search for Resources


Using Search Bar from Homepage

  1. Click the Magnifying Glass icon on the header of the homepage to open the Search Bar.

  2. Enter your search term(s), if any, into the Search Bar.

  3. Press Enter on your keyboard.

    4. Note: If you are unsure of what you are searching for, you can leave the Search Bar empty and press Enter to return your search results.


Add Search Filters

  1. On the Search page, select the Subject and Level from the dropdown list.

  2. Click Apply and choose Apply Filter to narrow down the search results.

  3. To add more search filters, click Filter.

  4. Under Location, select from:

    • All

    • My Drive - your own resources and those shared with you

    • Community Gallery - Lessons/Courses shared by teachers

    • MOE Library - Resources produced by MOE

    • App Library - App Resources integrated with SLS

  5. Under Resource (Resource Type), select from:

    • All

    • Lessons & Courses

    • Quizzes

    • Questions (or Question Types)

    • Media (or Media Types)

    • Apps

  6. Under Subject, select from:

    • Assigned Subjects

    • Followed Subjects (if any)

    • Other Subjects

  7. Under Levels, select from:

    • Assigned Levels

    • Followed Levels (if any)

    • Other Levels

  8. Click Apply and select Apply Filters to perform your selection.

Add Topic Filters

  1. You may also select Add Topic Filters to filter according to Topics or Sub-topics.

    Note: You may search for Resources based on Topics or Sub-topics if they have been tagged to a content map.

  2. To add topic filters, select one Subject and one Level from the dropdown list.

  3. Click Apply and choose Add Topic Filters.

  4. On the Add Topic Filters subpage, select the relevant Content Map dropdown list.

  5. Select the checkbox to the left of the Topic, Subtopics, or Learning Outcome to narrow down the search results.


View Search Results

  1. You can sort the resources by Relevance to your search term(s) or according to the date of the latest edit.

  2. Click the Card or the Open button. You will be brought to the Lesson or Course page. Alternatively, click Lesson/Course Details to view the Lesson/Course Details.

  3. Click the Overflow (3-dot icon) found on the top right of the Lesson/Course Card to copy the Lesson/Course to My Drive.
