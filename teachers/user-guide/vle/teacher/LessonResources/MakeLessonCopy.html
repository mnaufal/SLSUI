Make a Copy of Lesson/Course


After selecting a MOE Library or Community Gallery Lesson/Course that you would like to adapt for use, you can make a copy of it before editing and assigning to your students.

  1. Click the Lesson or Course Card to view the Lesson or Course.

  2. On the Lesson or Course page, click the Overflow (3-dot icon) and click Copy to My Drive.

  3. You will be prompted to:

    • Rename the Lesson or Course, and

    • Choose the destination folder in My Drive

  4. Click Copy when done.

  5. Alternatively, you can copy the Lesson or Course from the Lesson or Course Cards in your Search Results by clicking the Overflow (3-dot icon) and click Copy To.

  6. You can then access the copied Lesson or Course from the listing in My Drive. To view the author or source of the resource in My Drive, click the Overflow (3-dot icon) and select View Details.

Note: For MOE Library Resources, you will only be able to copy a Lesson or Course to My Drive if permission to copy had been enabled.
