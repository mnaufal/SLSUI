Submit Lesson/Course

If you have a Lesson or Course that you would like to share with teachers on SLS, you may submit it to the Community Gallery. This includes gamified Courses.

Lesson or Course Submission Checklist:

  • Update Lesson or Course Description to give other users an overview of what the Lesson or Course is about. Tag your Lesson or Course with the appropriate Lesson/Course Tag.

  • Ensure that collaborative features (eg. Padlet, Mentimeter, Nearpod, etc.) and documents (eg. Google Docs, Google Slides, etc.) have read-only rights because embedded content can be viewed by all.

  • Ensure that your Lesson or Course meets Copyright guidelines

Your submission will be featured after it has been approved by one of the Community Gallery Administrators (CG Admins).


Tagging a Lesson or Course

Before submitting any Lesson or Course to the Community Gallery, tag your Lesson or Course. You may refer to Tag a Lesson or Course for more information.


Check Lesson for Submission

  1. Open the Lesson or Course that you wish to submit to the Community Gallery.

  2. Click the Overflow (3-dot icon) on the top right of the header, and click Check for Submission (Community Gallery).

  3. You will be prompted to check that the content of the Lesson or Course is in line with the Community Gallery guidelines.

  4. If the Lesson is Ready for Submission, check the box to acknowledge that the content complies with SLS Terms of Use and the Copyright Guidelines, followed by Submit.

    Note: To make any edits before submission, close the pop-up and click the Pencil icon to make any changes.

  5. On the same subpage, you may wish to rename the Lesson or Course Title, and edit the Permission Settings by selecting the checkboxes beside:

    • Allow teachers to copy/assign/publish and students to copy this Lesson/Course

    • Allow teachers and students to view print-friendly worksheet

    • Allow students to reattempt Lesson/Course as self-study

  6. Once done, click Submit to proceed. After submitting to Community Gallery, a notification message will indicate that you have submitted a copy of your Lesson or Course for approval.

  7. The Community Gallery Administrators will either approve your submission or return it, following which, you will receive a notification informing you about the outcome.
