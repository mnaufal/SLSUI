Copy Lesson/Course within My Drive

You can make a copy of an existing Resource (Course and Lesson) under My Lessons & Courses tab.

  1. To make a copy of an existing Resource, click the Overflow (3-dot icon) and click Make a Copy.

  2. In the Make a copy of a Lesson or Course subpage, select the folder to copy the Resources to. By default, the My Drive folder is chosen for you. You can also create a New Folder if necessary.

  3. Click Copy.
