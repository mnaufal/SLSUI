Manage My Templates (New)

You can now save Lesson and Course Templates for quick Lesson or Course authoring.

Adding a New Template

  1. Open the left menu from the header at the homepage. Click Resources, followed by My Drive. By default, you will be in the My Lessons & Courses tab.

  2. Select My Templates to view your saved Lesson or Course Templates.

  3. To add a new template, click Add New Template.

  4. For Lesson Templates, select the number of Activities, followed by Create. For Course Templates, select the number of Sections and Activities within each Section, followed by Create.

  5. Enter the title of the Lesson or Course and click Create.

  6. The Lesson or Course Plan will be shown.

  7. Lesson or Course Templates are indicated by a grey banner below the Lesson or Course Editor header.

Note: Instead of creating a new template from scratch, you can also use an existing Lesson or Course as a template by selecting Set as Template from the right menu.


Editing a Template

  1. To edit a Template, click Open under the Template Title. You will be brought to the Lesson or Course Editor.

  2. Click the Pencil icon to make the necessary changes.

  3. When done, click the Done icon to save your changes.
