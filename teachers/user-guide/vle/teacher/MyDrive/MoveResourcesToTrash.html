Move Resources to Trash

Resources (Lessons and Courses) can be deleted if you no longer need them.

  1. Open the left menu from the header on the homepage. Click Resources, followed by My Drive. By default, you will be in the My Lessons & Courses tab.

  2. In the listing, click the Overflow (3-dot icon) followed by Move to Trash.

  3. You can move more than one Resource into the Trash in one go. Select the Resources using the checkboxes on the left and click Move to Trash.

  4. You may also click Move to Trash after opening up a Lesson or Course. Open the right menu and select Move to Trash.
