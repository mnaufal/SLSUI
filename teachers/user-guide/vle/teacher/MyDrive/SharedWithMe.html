View Resources Shared with Me (Improved)

Resources (Lessons and Courses) shared with you can now be accessed from My Drive.

  1. Open the left menu from the header at the homepage. Click Resources, followed by My Drive. By default, you will be in the My Lessons & Courses tab.

  2. To view the Resources teachers have shared with you, click Shared With Me.

  3. To search for the Resources shared with you, enter your search term(s) in the Search Shared With Me search bar.

  4. Press "Enter" to view your search results.

  5. Results to the search term(s) will be listed. You can sort by Title, Type, and Last Modified through the header.

  6. For Resources under Shared With Me, click the Overflow (3-dot icon) to Make a Copy or View Details.

  7. To return to My Drive, click My Drive.


Deleting a Shared Resource a Template

  1. To delete a shared Resource, enter the Resource.

  2. Open the right menu and select Share with Teachers.

  3. Find your name in the Share with Teachers subpage and click Delete.

  4. Click Save to confirm the changes.

  5. The shared Resource will no longer be visible under your Shared with Me.
