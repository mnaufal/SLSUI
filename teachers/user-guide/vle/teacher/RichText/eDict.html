Localisation and E-Dictionary

  1. To create a more immersive MTL experience for lessons and courses, click Edit to access Lesson/Course Details.

  2. Click the dropdown under Language Localisation and E-Dictionary, and select the relevant option.

  3. When the E-Dictionary feature is enabled, students will have an enhanced annotation feature. They may view the meaning of a selected word or phrase, and add their search results to the right drawer as notes.

  4. If there are no further changes, click Save.
